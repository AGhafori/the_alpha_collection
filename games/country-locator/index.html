🌍 Know Your Continents

Each game starts with a random continent in focus. You will see a country outline and must type the country's name.

✅ Correct — +10 points and the country is revealed on the map.
❌ Wrong — the answer is counted as a miss and the game moves on.
🧭 Goal — reveal as many countries as you can in the chosen continent.
